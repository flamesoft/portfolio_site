It's a rainy Friday

To live in Gothenburg, you must equip yourself with the best stuff you can find which is water-proof and wind-proof.

Buy the most expensive one with good brands, it is just a necessary investment for you to survive in Gothenburg, highly recommended in the "Gothenburg Survival Handbook" which I am going to write when I become a pensioner.

In a rainy day as today, you definitely need a pair of rubber boots and rain coats. Then maybe prepare some good food and popcorn for TV time on your sofa. This is the poor entertainment we will have today.
